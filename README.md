# Ecological factors influencing male precociality in Chinook salmon parr

## Lead Author
Loren Stearman
Loren.Stearman@noaa.gov

## Coauthors/Collaborators
Jesse J Lamb


## Research Questions
- What spatial or environmental factors seem to encourage precocious male parr in Chinook salmon in the wild?
- How do these factors compare to or differ from well-studied factors encouraging precociality in hatchery fish?


## Dataset(s)
All production datasets are currently in assembly. This repository currently holds data assembly scripts. Data listed below are planned for analyses. 

#### Idaho Wild Fish Juvenile Chinook Salmon PIT-Tagging Data
- Abundance, length, mass, and precocial status data for all Chinook salmon parr, 2001 - 2025

#### PIT Tag Information System (PTAGIS)
- Detections on in-stream arrays at or near natal rearing habitats, 2001 - 2025. 

#### National Hydrography Dataset V2
- Multiple measures of watershed and catchment morphology

#### National Land Cover Database
- Land use/land cover for all upstream catchments in study from 2001 - 2024

#### Pacific Northwest Predicted Stream Temperatures
- Modeled stream temperatures for all sites in study from 2001 - 2021

#### Fish Passage Center Adult Counts
- Adult counts for Spring/Summer Chinook Salmon, 2000 - 2025
