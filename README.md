## Research Questions
- What spatial or environmental factors seem to encourage precocious male parr in Chinook salmon in the wild?
- How do these factors compare to or differ from well-studied factors encouraging precociality in hatchery fish?


## Dataset(s)
All production datasets are currently in assembly. This repository currently holds data assembly scripts. Data listed below are planned for analyses. 

#### Idaho Wild Fish Juvenile Chinook Salmon PIT-Tagging Data
- Access: Currently on request from the authors
- Abundance, length, mass, and precocial status data for all Chinook salmon parr, 2001 - 2025

#### PIT Tag Information System (PTAGIS)
- Access: [PTAGIS](https://www.ptagis.org)
- Specific queries/subsets: Queried for projects with codes SA (Steve Achord) or GAA (Gordon Axel), and for detections in HUC6 170602 (Salmon)
- Variables used: detections on in-stream arrays at or near natal rearing habitats, 2001 - 2025. 

#### National Hydrography Dataset V2
- Access: [NHDPlusV2 via USEPA](https://www.epa.gov/waterdata/get-nhdplus-national-hydrography-dataset-plus-data)
- Specific queries/subsets: Data downloaded from Pacific Northwest (HUC2 17)
- Variables used: multiple measures of watershed and catchment morphology

#### National Land Cover Database Annual
- Access: [Multi-Resource Land Characteristics Consortium (MRLC)](https://www.mrlc.gov/data/project/annual-nlcd)
- Variables used: land use/land cover for all upstream catchments in study from 2001 - 2024

#### Pacific Northwest Predicted Stream Temperatures
- Access: [Columbia Data Access in Real-Time (DART)](https://www.cbr.washington.edu/shiny/salmon-stream-temperature/)
- Specific queries/subsets: Data extracted for HUC6 170602 (Salmon)
- Variables used: modeled stream temperatures for all sites in study from 2001 - 2021

#### Fish Passage Center Adult Counts
- Access: [Columbia Data Access in Real-Time (DART)](https://www.cbr.washington.edu/dart/query/adult_daily)
- Alternate Access: [Fish Passage Center](https://www.fpc.org/adults/Q_adults_passagedata.php)
- Specific queries/subsets: Filtered for spring/summer Chinook at Lower Granite Dam
- Variables used: adult counts for Spring/Summer Chinook Salmon, 2000 - 2025
